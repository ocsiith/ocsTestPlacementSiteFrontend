Recruiters Policy for Internships

Structure and Rules for the Internship Process

  1. The OCS follows a strict "One Student, One Job Policy". Once a student is offered a job, he/she is out of the internship process.

  2. In case a candidate receives multiple offers, the candidate has to make a choice at the end of the slot. The companies will be intimated about the final choice of the candidate two hours after the end of the slot, or receipt of the offers, whichever is later.

  3. Companies are always advised to be in touch with the Internship volunteers and staff.

  4. The 2 months internship should be held during the period starting from mid-week of May 2023 to the 3rd week of July 2023.

  5. Internship process shall be completed on a slot basis. Each company has been allotted one of the three slots on a particular day. The slot timings are as follows:

    1. Slot 1: 07:00 to 12:00

    2. Slot 2: 13:00 to 18:00

    3. Slot 3: 19:00 to 00:00

  6. The dates are allotted based on the information provided in the Internship registration form (IRF) submitted by the companies while considering several parameters, including the following not necessarily in any order of priority:

    1. Job profile and career growth prospects

    2. Branch specification given by the company

    3. Likely number of offers this year

    4. Student ratings

    5. Alumni feedback

    6. General research on industry trends

  7. The 6 months internship should be starting from January 2023 to June 2023.

  8. All preplacement offers extended to any student have to be routed through the OCS.

  9. Any form of intimation from an organization about the possibility of a PPO being granted in future should be informed to the Placement Office without waiting for the formal offer letter/email. The Placement Office will take up the matter with the organization further and the student will be allowed to appear for on campus placement activities as per normal policy until a decision regarding the grant of a PPO is made by the organization.

  10. During the Internship, the company should not terminate the students without valid reasons and do not stop the stipend. Then the company should be backlisted in the next year.

  11. Perhaps, if the company terminates the student(s) with a valid reason, the company has to issue a certificate until the student(s) have been working in the organization.


For all matters not covered by the above stated, the Office of Career Services will use its own discretion to make appropriate decisions from time to time and case to case. Moreover, the Institute has full discretion to change/modify the rules from time to time based on the students interest and objective of the OCS.

We look forward to a smooth process and a long-term relationship with the industry.
